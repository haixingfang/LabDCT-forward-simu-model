checkZeroRange

(method of zeroRangeMethod)

Syntax

isZero = checkZeroRange(zrm,v,i) % check at position in the i-th pole figure

Input

zrm

zeroRangeMethod
ori

orientation
v

vector3d
i

check in the i~th pole figure

Output

isZero

boolean vector of length(ori)
